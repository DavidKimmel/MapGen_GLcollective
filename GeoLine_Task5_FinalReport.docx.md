## **3.3 Quality Gaps (The Real Opportunity)**

Their catalog is vast but the execution is shallow. These are quality deficiencies GeoLine can exploit on every shared city:

* Low resolution street detail — labels barely readable at thumbnail scale; GeoLine's GIS pipeline renders at 300+ DPI

* Flat, undifferentiated color palette — light blue water, gray streets, muted green on white is functional but uninspired

* Generic map extents — awkward crops that don't honor each city's actual geographic shape or most interesting area

* No cartographic design hierarchy — all features weighted equally, no intentional stroke variation or label priority

* Manual custom order workflow — 'preview sent before shipping' introduces delays; GeoLine's pipeline can show instant preview

* Stale shop branding — announcement last updated July 2023; no active brand presence or storytelling
